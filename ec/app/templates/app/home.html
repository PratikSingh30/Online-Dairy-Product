Products

Dairy products are food products made from or containing milk.
The most common dairy animals are cow, water buffalo, nanny goat, and ewe.
Some popular dairy products include milk, yogurt, cheese, butter, cream, condensed milk, evaporated milk, milk powder, whey, and casein.
Dairy products are a good source of protein, calcium, and other nutrients.
They can be a part of a healthy diet for most people.
However, some people may need to avoid dairy products due to lactose intolerance or other health conditions.
